Are you IT literate?


10/07/2025


Youtuber ThioJoe made a video to test if a person is a computer expert or not.

I studied the contents, and turned it into a quiz in this page, just for fun!

I rephrase it as "are you IT literate?"

Only 44 questions. So, ready when you are.


Your score


1. Do you know what Operating System you're using?


2. Do you know what version Operating System you're using?


3. Do you know what this is?


4. Are you someone who asks others for computer help?


5. Are you someone who gives computer help?


6. Are you a hunt-and-peck typer? (meaning: you don't know how to properly type on the keyboard)


7. Do you know how big your hard drive is? (in gigabytes)


8. Do you know your current screen resolution?


9. Do you know how many cores your CPU has?


10. Do you still use your computer's default browser?


11. Have you ever opened up your computer case?


12. Have you ever used Linux?


13. Do you back up your hard drive?


14. Do you know whether your computer is Intel or AMD?


15. Have you ever changed your browser homepage?


Alright now things might start picking up a little bit...

16. Have you ever changed your Wi-Fi password from the default?


17. Do you know what USB stands for?


18. Do you know the difference between a megabit and a megabyte?


19. How many hours do you spend on the computer daily on average? (not counting when you are working)


20. Have you ever used the command prompt?


21. Do you know the difference between Java and Javascript?


22. Do your passwords contain special characters?


23. Have you ever installed new hardware in your computer?


24. Do you know the difference between HTTP and HTTPS?


25. Have you ever built a computer?


26. Have you ever overclocked your CPU manually?


27. Do you know what RAM timings are and how they work?


28. Tick if you know these famous computer people :


29. Do you know what the Insert key does?


30. Do you know what the scroll lock key does?


31. Have you not had a computer virus in the past 6 months?


32. Do you not need Antivirus?


33. Do you encrypt your hard drive?


34. Do you have a home server?


35. Have you ever edited the Windows registry?


36. Can you write a "Hello world" program? (any programming language)


37. Have you ever spent more than 1/3 of your monthly income on a computer part? (money from your parents doesn't count)


38. Do you know about Google Ultron?


39. Aside from default web browser, how many additional web browsers you have installed?


You're almost there...

40. Have you ever made your own Ethernet cable? (cut it up, add it on RJ45 pin, crimp everything)


41. Have you ever installed a custom firmware on your router?


42. Have you ever resized a drive partition?


43. Have you ever used SSH to remotely access a computer?


44. Do you use custom DNS servers? (either just in the computer or on your router)


FINISH, now check your score below
