I thought Cybertrooper isn't real

and I didn't know it was exclusively a Malaysian thing


10 Jan 2026



I'm sure you noticed this too.

One day you browse Facebook. Your feeds are saturated with memes and AI slops. Meanwhile MIM, PHM and "Madani" thing pops up at a usual amount. You occassionally laugh at Padapoik or MGAG, or got lost in Reels infinitely.

It's a normal day in Facebook if that's the case. Until you stumble upon and look closer at one or two random posts about Malaysian Prime Minister, Anwar Ibrahim (or PMX for short).

You can't just read titles. You have to check the sources and facts. Sometimes, you have to enter the comments section.

You will find a phenomena not found elsewhere on the globe. The cybertroopers and their comments.
