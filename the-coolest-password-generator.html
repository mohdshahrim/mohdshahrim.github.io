Why you should use strong password?

Separating between you and malicious hackers is a thin barrier called "password". You can't stop hackers from crossing the barrier, but you can make it not so easy for them (at least on your part). The stronger the password, the thicker the barrier, hence the harder it is to crack.

Do yourself a favor. Use strong password.
